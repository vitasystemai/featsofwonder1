The Difference is in the Details.

Parents choose Feats of Wonder because they see extraordinary results. We have meticulously crafted an environment where children don't just learn—they thrive.

An Innovative, Child-Centric Curriculum

Our approach is designed for modern learners. Forget heavy, cumbersome backpacks. We utilize a unique, all-in-one concept book that holistically covers all subjects, making learning integrated and easy for your child to manage. Our days are filled with enriching activities, from productive, play-based learning and vital social interaction to exciting summer camps and weekly water activities, ensuring your child is always engaged and joyful.

A Nurturing Environment That Feels Like Family

Our teaching staff, including celebrated educators like Anjali Ma'am and Prasanna Ma'am, are the heart of our school. They are masters at nurturing and developing not just academic skills, but the whole child. As a parent, you will find our team to be exceptionally friendly, approachable, and genuinely invested in your child's happiness and progress.

Uncompromising Standards of Safety & Hygiene

Your child’s well-being is our absolute priority. We pride ourselves on the immaculate neatness and hygiene of our school, both inside and outside the premises. From our infrastructure to our daily routines, we maintain the highest standards to provide a safe and healthy space for your little one to explore.

Leadership That Listens and Acts

We are led by a hands-on founder who personally ensures excellence in every detail—from the quality of books and uniforms to addressing parent concerns with valuable, actionable advice. This meticulous attention to detail is what sets us apart and ensures a smooth, flawless experience for both children and parents.

Leadership with Passion and Global Expertise

The heart and vision of Feats of Wonder come from our founder, Yoshitha.

Her unparalleled dedication and deep understanding of education are the cornerstones of our school's success.

With an MBA in HR and over 15 years of experience in various international schools, Yoshitha has a profound understanding of what it takes to deliver a truly holistic education. Her impressive career includes:

  • 10 years as a dedicated teacher in primary and secondary schools.
  • 5 years in Head positions for leading international preschools and primary schools.
  • Successfully managing and mentoring over 500 students, guiding them toward academic excellence and personal growth.
  • Holding prestigious certifications in Child and Adolescent Development & Emotional Wellness/Mind Coaching from NHCA Singapore.
  • Recipient of the "Best Mentor" award at the Bridge Intelia Young Scientist Talent Virtual Fair, a testament to her commitment to fostering young talent.

We are proud to be led by a founder whose vision, expertise, and dedication are shaping the future of education.

Our Guiding Principles

Our mission is clear and woven into everything we do. We are committed to:

  • Fostering Holistic Growth: To support every child's development—cognitive, social, emotional, and physical—in a safe, respectful, and wonderfully inclusive environment.
  • Igniting Natural Curiosity: To cultivate creativity, confidence, and a love for learning through dynamic, play-based learning and meaningful interactions.
  • Preparing for Life's Journey: To equip children with the essential skills they need for a successful transition to primary school and for life itself.
  • Building a Strong Community: To actively engage families in the learning process, promoting a culture of partnership, respect, and diversity.
